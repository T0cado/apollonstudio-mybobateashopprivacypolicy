This privacy policy explains how Apollon Studio (hereinafter “we”, “our”) uses and stores various personal data that we collect about you when you use our applications

  • Information that you provide by asking for help from our support service (for example, chat posts and support requests).
This data is collected using special tools (Software Development Kit or “SDK” provided by our partners, which are an indispensable part of the application.

Use our applications;

Transfer of information to third parties:
We will not sell or distribute your personal data to third parties if we do not have your permission or this is not required by law.

What we do to protect your personal data
We strive to ensure the security of your personal data. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of information, we have established appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to protect and protect the information we receive from you.
How long do we keep your personal data We store your data as long as necessary to ensure that you can use our applications.
